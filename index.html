About StudentHub

NE India's student economy, built by students.

Welcome! We’re so glad you’re here. Step into a friendly and supportive community where students and individuals come together to share knowledge, skills, and real-life experiences. We believe that everyone has something valuable to offer, and here, your knowledge can not only help others grow but also become a source of income for you. The idea is simple: college students are already teaching, training, and selling. StudentHub gives them a credible, structured place to do it — with clean profiles, transparent pricing, and direct WhatsApp communication that keeps things fast and personal.

Whether you're looking for academic support, practical guidance, or new skills, you’ll find people ready to help. Users can explore a wide range of study-related courses, while those passionate about teaching can step in as tutors by creating and offering their own courses.

Beyond academics, our platform opens doors to diverse learning opportunities. From fitness coaching to design and other professional skills, our experts are here to guide you with their experience so you can achieve the best results.

We also bring adventure to your fingertips. Plan and book trips with your friends to explore the beautiful landscapes of North East India. Our experienced guides are excited to accompany you, ensuring a memorable and enriching travel experience.

Don’t miss out on the opportunity to learn, earn, and explore. Click “Join” and become a part of our growing community today.
